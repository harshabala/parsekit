# SOUL.md – Design Philosophy
1. Hide complexity, never hide power.
2. Make the refinery invisible — feels like a native macOS feature.
3. Defaults that feel like magic (OCR on, smart Markdown, sensible naming).
4. Local-first & trust-maximizing — proudly communicate privacy.
5. Clarity over cleverness — human language everywhere.
6. Composable & future-proof — open formats only.

Tone: calm, confident, practical. No hype, no jargon.
